Pikachu

Pikachu (Japanese: ピカチュウ, Hepburn: Pikachuu) (PEE-ka-choo[1]) is an Electric-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. Pikachu is renowned for being the most well-known and recognizable Pokémon. Over the years, Pikachu has become so popular that it serves as the Pokémon franchise mascot. It is the Version Mascot and First partner Pokémon for the game Pokémon Yellow and its remake, Pokémon: Let's Go, Pikachu!. It is also well known from the anime, where Ash Ketchum, the former protagonist, owns a Pikachu.
